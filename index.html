About me

I recently graduated International Game Architecture and Design at Breda University of Applied Sciences earning my Bachelor of Applied Sciences. During this time I gained experience in a wide variety of programming roles such as graphics engineering on various devices including hand held consoles and cutting edge desktop hardware.
Later on I interned at a research group where I worked on the technology and tools required for a multi disciplinary Virtual Reality project. Here I also worked on developing and improving tools used for photorealistic virtual human avatars.

Apart from programming I enjoy spending time on exercising like sports climbing, bouldering, mountain biking or windsurfing. During moment of physical relaxation I can likely be found playing roleplaying, racing or shooter games which are also my favourite ways to socialise and stay in touch with friends.
